About

  • I started my journey of being a web developer in the early 2020's, at the age of 33. Since then, I have written several hundred lines of code. I am from Hungary and really thrilled to become a full-proof webdev.
  • Of course, there was a simple website project I did when I was a teenager as a gig for a fellow student in my school, but since then I wasn't getting busy with web development in any way.
  • So, around March 2020, I started out with an online webdev bootcamp and self-learning from FCC and some very important online reference sites, such as MDN and CSS-Tricks.
  • Before learning to become a webdev, I have been working in automotive industry and warehouses. Gradually, it came to me, that I need to make a change and do what I love and enjoy the most. Here I am right now.
